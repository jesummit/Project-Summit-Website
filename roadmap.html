What's being built

A platform that keeps getting smarter.

Here's where Project Summit stands today, and where it's headed. No vague promises — just a clear picture of the plan.

Now available On the App Store

iOS App — v1.0

The full core platform. Adaptive load management, intelligent recovery, race-day nutrition, and structured workouts — all in one place.

🫀
HRV-Driven Recovery
Reads HRV, resting HR & sleep from Apple Health. Adjusts daily intensity before you open the app.
🗓
Advanced Periodisation
Base, build, peak and taper blocks structured around your target events.
🔁
Plan Cascading
Skipped and completed sessions automatically trigger plan adjustments.
🧩
Adaptation to Availability
Illness, injury, mental readiness, and schedule changes reshape the plan in real time.
⚡
eFTP & Power Curve
Continuous estimation via the CP2 model. No manual tests — your rides do the work.
📈
CTL / ATL / TSB Real Time
Training load, fatigue, and form tracked live across every session.
🔗
Strava Sync & intervals.icu
Activities imported from Strava. Workouts distributed via intervals.icu to Garmin, Zwift & MyWhoosh.
🍽️
Race Day Nutrition Planner
Carb, fluid and electrolyte targets built around your race duration and intensity.
◦
Coming Next Late 2026

Wearables, Pacing & Wellness

Bringing the platform closer to your wrist, your race strategy, and your overall health picture.

❤️
HR-Only Training Zones
Structured workouts prescribed in heart rate zones for athletes without a power meter.
📆
Training-Driven Nutrition Plan
Daily fueling built from your planned and completed workouts — carb targets that rise on hard days and ease on recovery days — with daily recipes and an auto-generated shopping list.
⌚
Apple Watch App & Complication
Recovery score, today's load, and workout summary right on your wrist.
🏁
Race-Day Pacing Strategies
Power targets and segment breakdowns tailored to your current fitness and race profile.
💊
Wellness Tracking
Log sleep quality, mood, soreness, and stress alongside your training data.
