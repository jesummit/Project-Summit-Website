01 What Summit is

Project Summit ("Summit", "the App", "we", "us") is an adaptive cycling training platform for iOS developed by Jordi Espanyol, an independent developer based in Spain.

Summit connects your training data, health metrics, and nutrition to generate personalised cycling training plans that adapt in real time to your body and your life.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to these Terms. If you don't agree, please don't use Summit.

03 Subscription & billing

Summit offers a Free tier and Summit Premium, which includes Pro and Elite subscription plans. Pricing and features for each tier are shown in the App and on our website.

Billing. Summit Premium subscriptions (Pro and Elite) are billed monthly or annually through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment processing — we never see your card details.

Cancellation. You can cancel anytime from your Apple ID settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial periods, but Apple's standard refund policy applies.

Changes to pricing. If we change Summit Premium pricing, we'll give you at least 30 days' notice. Your subscription continues at the original price until your renewal date.

Beta period. During the current beta and early-launch period, access may be provided free of charge. We'll communicate clearly before any paid tier (Summit Premium) goes live.

Important. Summit is a training tool, not a medical device. The information it provides — including training load recommendations, recovery scores, and nutrition targets — is based on sports science models and your personal data. It is not medical advice.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any new training programme, especially if you have existing health conditions, injuries, or concerns.

Summit's training recommendations are designed for healthy adults engaged in recreational cycling. They are not intended for people with serious cardiovascular conditions, eating disorders, or other conditions where intense exercise may be contraindicated.

If you experience pain, dizziness, shortness of breath, or any other concerning symptoms during training, stop immediately and seek medical attention.

By using Summit, you acknowledge that you train at your own risk and that we are not liable for any injury or health outcome arising from your use of the App.
