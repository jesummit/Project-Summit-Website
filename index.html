What Project Summit does

01

Adaptive Training Load

Tracks CTL, ATL, and TSS in real time. Detects skipped sessions and automatically cascades plan adjustments so you never follow a stale schedule.

02

FTP & Power Curve Intelligence

Continuously estimates your eFTP and power zones using the CP2 model. No manual tests needed — your training data speaks for itself.

03

HRV-Driven Recovery

Reads your HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep quality from Apple Health. Adjusts daily intensity before you even open the app.

04

Push Workouts Everywhere

Structured workouts flow directly to Garmin, Zwift, and MyWhoosh via intervals.icu — one tap, ready to ride.
