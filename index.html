What's in v1.0

01

CTL / ATL / TSB — Real Time

Training load, fatigue, and form tracked live across every session. The numbers that serious cyclists actually use, always current.

02

HRV-Driven Recovery

Reads your HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep from Apple Health. Intensity adjusts before you even open the app.

03

eFTP & Power Curve

Continuous estimation via the CP2 model. No manual tests needed — your ride history does the work.

04

Advanced Periodisation

Base, build, peak, and taper blocks structured around your target events — with full adaptation to your real-life availability.

05

Plan Cascading

Skipped sessions, illness, injury, or a change in schedule — the plan reshapes itself around you. No more stale training blocks.

06

Push Workouts via intervals.icu

Structured sessions distributed to Garmin, Zwift, and MyWhoosh in one tap. Rides imported automatically from Strava.

Daily Nutrition Planning

Is this for you?

Made for the serious amateur.

Project Summit is opinionated. It's built for a specific type of cyclist — one who wants real training science, not a watered-down experience.

  • You train with a power meter and take your FTP seriously
  • You already use Strava and want your data to actually work for you
  • You've outgrown generic plans and want something that adapts to your life
  • You care about recovery as much as volume
  • You have a target event and want to peak for it
  • You want nutrition integrated into your training, not bolted on
