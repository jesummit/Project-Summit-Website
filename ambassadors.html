Summit Ambassadors

Ride it. Train with it. Tell the world it works.

We're looking for cyclists who take their training seriously and aren't shy about sharing it. This is a new programme — small, personal, and built around real athletes.

Cyclists who train seriously and talk about it.

We're not looking for influencers with a product-placement mindset. We're looking for athletes who genuinely care about performance — and whose communities trust them because of it.

  • You train consistently and track your performance
  • You have an active presence on social media — Instagram, Strava, YouTube, or similar
  • Your audience is made up of cyclists who take riding seriously
  • You talk honestly about your training — the good sessions and the hard ones
  • You're keen to help shape a product you use and give real, honest feedback

What you get

🚀

A direct line

A direct line to the team and an early look at what's coming next — plus real influence on where Summit goes.

⭐

Free Elite account

Full access to Summit Premium Elite — every feature, for as long as you're part of the programme.

🤝

Collaboration

Content collaboration, mentions, and the chance to shape how Summit is communicated to the cycling community.

How it works

01

Write to us

Send a short email to jordi@projectsummit.app introducing yourself. Tell us who you are, how you train, and where your community lives. No forms, no decks — just a real message.

02

We'll have a conversation

If it looks like a good fit, we'll reply and get to know each other. This is a small programme — we'd rather have five great ambassadors than fifty nominal ones.

03

Get set up and start training

You'll get an Elite account and a direct channel with the team. Use Summit for your real training, share honestly, and help us build something worth talking about.
